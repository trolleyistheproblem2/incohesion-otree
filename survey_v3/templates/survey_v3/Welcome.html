Welcome! This is an experiment by the Center for Experimental Social Sciences. You are invited to answer some questions and play 3 games where your responses to different decision situations influence your final earnings. Your earnings during the experiment will depend on your decisions, and the decisions of the other participants.

You will play 3 games. At the end of the study, the computer will randomly pick any 2 of these games to make your payments.

In total, the experiment will take approximately 45 minutes. All instructions will be provided on your screens.

Before we begin the game, we will ask you to answer a short questionnaire, starting with your subject ID. This is the number written on the chit you picked out of the bag, before entering the room.
